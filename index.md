## Biography

Xiangyu Zhao is now a Master student studying in School of Biomedical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. Zhao received his 
B.E. degree from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in June 2021, and was awarded as a Beijing Excellent Graduate 
(top 5%). He joined the Medical Image Computing Lab in the School of Biomedical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong 
University, under the supervision of Dr. Lichi Zhang.  
Zhao's research interests include segmentation and registration of medical images, few shot learning and semi-supervised learning in medical images, and the knowledge transfer of foundation models in computer vision to medical domain.
