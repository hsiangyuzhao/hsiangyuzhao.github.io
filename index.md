## Biography

Xiangyu Zhao is now a Master student studying in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. Zhao received his 
B.E. degree from Beihang University, Beijing, China in June 2021, and was awarded as a Beijing Excellent Graduate 
(top 5%). He joined the Medical Image Computing Lab in the School of Biomedical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong 
University in the same year, under the supervision of Dr. Lichi Zhang.  
Zhao's research interests include segmentation and registration of medical images, few shot medical image segmentation
and semi-supervised learning in medical images.
